How to be proper

Basic Etiquette for a Gentleman greeting a lady Acquaintance

  • Wait for her acknowledging bow before tipping your hat
  • use the hand farthest from her to raise
  • When walking, the lady must always have the wall.

Recourse for a lady towards unpleeasant men who persist in Bowing

  1. A simple stare of iviness should suffice in most instance
  2. A cold bow discourage familiarity without offeiring insult.
  3. As a last resort ,"sir I have not the honour of your Acquaintance.
